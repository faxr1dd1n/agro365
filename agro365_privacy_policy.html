"Agro365" ilovasi (keyingi o'rinlarda "Ilova") foydalanuvchilar maxfiyligini hurmat qiladi. Ushbu Maxfiylik Siyosati ilova to'playdigan ma'lumotlar, ulardan foydalanish tartibi va foydalanuvchi huquqlari haqida ma'lumot beradi.

2. To'planadigan ma'lumotlar

  • Joylashuv ma'lumotlari - xarita va yaqin obyektlarni ko'rsatish funksiyalari uchun.
  • Kamera va galereya - profil rasmi, hujjat yoki foto yuklash uchun.
  • Qurilma ma'lumotlari - ilova versiyasi, qurilma turi, OS versiyasi.
  • Internet trafigi - ilova Agro365 serverlariga ulanadi.

3. Ma'lumotlardan foydalanish maqsadi

  • Ilovaning asosiy funksiyalarini ta'minlash
  • Foydalanuvchi tajribasini yaxshilash
  • Texnik nosozliklarni aniqlash va bartaraf etish
  • Xavfsizlikni ta'minlash

4. Ma'lumotlarni uchinchi shaxslarga uzatish

Biz foydalanuvchi ma'lumotlarini sotmaymiz, ijaraga bermaymiz va marketing maqsadida uchinchi shaxslarga uzatmaymiz. Ma'lumotlar faqat qonunchilik talab qilgan hollarda yoki xizmatni texnik ta'minlash uchun zarur hamkorlarga uzatilishi mumkin.

5. Ma'lumotlarni saqlash

Shaxsiy ma'lumotlar foydalanuvchi hisobi faol bo'lgan davrda yoki qonunchilikda belgilangan muddat davomida saqlanadi. Joylashuv ma'lumotlari funksional ehtiyojga ko'ra vaqtincha saqlanishi mumkin.

6. Foydalanuvchi huquqlari

  • Kamera va joylashuv ruxsatlarini istalgan vaqtda bekor qilish
  • Ma'lumotlarni ko'rish, yangilash yoki o'chirish bo'yicha murojaat qilish
  • Maxfiylik bo'yicha qo'llab-quvvatlash xizmatiga yozish

7. Bolalar maxfiyligi

Ilova 13 yoshdan kichik bolalarga mo'ljallanmagan. Biz bunday foydalanuvchilardan ma'lumotlarni ataylab to'plamaymiz.

8. O'zgarishlar

Ushbu siyosat vaqti-vaqti bilan yangilanishi mumkin. O'zgarishlar ushbu sahifada e'lon qilinadi.

9. Bog'lanish

1. Introduction

The "Agro365" app respects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and user rights.

2. Data We Collect

  • Location data - for map-based features and nearby object discovery.
  • Camera and gallery access - for uploading profile images, documents, or other media.
  • Device information - app version, device type, OS version.
  • Internet traffic - the app connects to Agro365 servers.

3. Purpose of Data Use

  • To provide core app functionality
  • To improve user experience
  • To detect and fix technical issues
  • To maintain service security

4. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share user data for marketing purposes. Data may be shared only when required by law, or with trusted technical service providers as needed to operate the app.

5. Data Retention

Personal data is retained while the account is active or as required by law. Location data may be stored temporarily where functionally required.

6. User Rights

  • Revoke camera/location permissions anytime in device settings
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of personal data
  • Contact support for privacy-related requests

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

For questions or complaints: support@agro365.uz
