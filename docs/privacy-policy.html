Agro365 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-02

Agro365 ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

  • Location data (for map and nearby service features)
  • Camera and gallery access (for uploading images or documents)
  • Device and technical data (app version, device model, OS version)
  • Network and usage data required to operate app services

Why We Use Data

  • To provide core app functionality
  • To improve product quality and user experience
  • To diagnose issues and maintain service security

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only when required by law or with trusted technical providers necessary to run Agro365 services.

Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active, or as required by applicable law.

Your Rights

  • You can revoke permissions (camera/location) at any time in device settings
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data

Children's Privacy

Agro365 is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@agro365.uz
